SwapIt Mobile App Now Available

September 20, 2025 SwapIt Team 4 min read

Today marks a major milestone for SwapIt as we officially launch our mobile application for both iOS and Android devices. Now you can browse, borrow, and manage all your transactions on the go!

Key Features

Our mobile app brings the full SwapIt experience to your pocket with enhanced features designed specifically for mobile users:

  • Instant Notifications: Get real-time alerts when someone requests your items or approves your borrowing request
  • Quick Scan: Use your camera to quickly add items by scanning barcodes or taking photos
  • Location-Based Search: Find items near you with GPS-enabled search
  • In-App Messaging: Chat directly with lenders and borrowers without leaving the app
  • Offline Mode: Browse your saved items and wishlists even without internet
