SwapIt Partners with Ashesi University

October 15, 2025 SwapIt Team 3 min read

We're thrilled to announce our official partnership with Ashesi University, marking a significant milestone in our mission to make sharing accessible and sustainable for students across Ghana.

What This Means for Students

Starting this semester, Ashesi students will have access to a dedicated SwapIt platform where they can:

  • Borrow textbooks and course materials for free or at reduced rates
  • Access campus equipment like projectors, cameras, and recording devices
  • Share transportation options for off-campus trips
  • Connect with peers for collaborative resource sharing

Building a Sustainable Campus

This partnership aligns with Ashesi's commitment to sustainability and community building. By encouraging students to share resources rather than purchase new items, we're reducing waste and making education more affordable.

"SwapIt represents exactly the kind of innovation we want to see at Ashesi. It combines technology, sustainability, and community values in a way that truly benefits our students."
- Dr. Patrick Awuah, Founder & President, Ashesi University

How to Get Started

Ashesi students can sign up using their university email address to access exclusive campus features. Simply visit our platform, create an account, and start browsing available items from fellow students and the university library.
